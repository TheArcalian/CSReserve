Make a resource request with the Civil Service Reserve

Before you start

The Civil Service Reserve helps to provide the expertise and capacity needed to respond quickly and effectively to crises that threaten our nation.

To request Reservists from the Civil Service Reserve, you will need:

  • to have registered as a host organisation with the Civil Service Reserve
  • sign in details for your Civil Service Reserve account
  • details for the role you are requesting, including the grade, profession, skills, security clearance, location and urgency

You can only make a request for one role at a time, if you require another role you must submit a separate request.
