Your Digital, Data and Technology Skills - Overview

We will now ask you to rate yourself against the Success Profiles

You must rate yourself against one of four levels:

  • Level 1: Awareness. You know about the skill and have an appreciation of how it is applied in the environment
  • Level 2: Working. You can apply your knowledge and experience of the skill, including tools and techniques. You can adopt those most appropriate for the environment
  • Level 3: Practitioner. You know how to share your knowledge and experience of this skill with others, including tools and techniques. You can define those most appropriate for the environment
  • Level 3: Expert. You have both knowledge and experience in the application of this skill. You are a recognised specialist and adviser in this skill including user needs, generation of ideas, methods and tools. You can lead or guide others in best-practice use
