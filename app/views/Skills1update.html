Your digital, data and technology skills - overview

We will now ask you to rate yourself against the digital, data and technology Profession Standards framework

You must rate yourself against one of five levels:

  • not applicable - not applicable to your role
  • awareness - you know about the skill and have an appreciation of how it is applied in the environment 
  • working - you can apply your knowledge and experience of the skill, including tools and techniques and you can adopt those most appropriate for the environment 
  • practitioner - you know how to share your knowledge and experience of this skill with others, including tools and techniques and you can define those most appropriate for the environment
  • expert - you have both knowledge and experience in the application of this skill, you are a recognised specialist and adviser in this skill including user needs, generation of ideas, methods and tools and you can lead or guide others in best-practice use

You must only rate yourself against the job families that are applicable to you.
