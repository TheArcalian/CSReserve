Your Policy Skills - Overview

We will now ask you to rate yourself against the Policy Profession Standards framework

You must rate yourself against one of three levels:

  • Level 1: Developing - Represents the baseline for Civil Servants and is relevant to roles at the HEO, SEO, Grade 7, Grade 6 or SCS1 grades
  • Level 2: Practitioner - As policy professionals progress they will have the opportunity to apply skills and knowledge and will become skilled practitioners, often leading policy areas and teams
  • Level 3: Expert - Policy professionals may progress from skilled practice to developing deep expertise for certain Standards, becoming policy leaders and sometimes undertaking professional qualifications
