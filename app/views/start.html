The Civil Service Reserve helps to provide the expertise and capacity needed to respond quickly and effectively to crises that threaten our nation.

The Civil Service Reserve supports the Civil Contingencies Secretariat, National Cyber Force, and Resilience and Recovery Directorate to manage unexpected crises, as well as known peaks in demands. It is made up of current and former civil servants who join on a voluntary basis and can be surged into particular roles in times of need.

Use this service to register with the Civil Service Reserve.

Start now

Before you start

To be eligible to join you must:

  • be a current or former civil servant
  • have obtained permission from your employer to join the Civil Service Reserve, if still in employment
  • be at grade HEO, SEO, Grade 7, Grade 6 or SCS1 if you are a current civil servant, if you are a former civil servant this should have been your most recent grade
  • have the right to work within the UK

Registering takes around 15 minutes.
