The Civil Service Reserve helps to provide the expertise and capacity needed to respond quickly and effectively to crises that threaten our nation.

The Civil Service Reserve supports the Civil Contingencies Secretariat, National Cyber Force, and Resilience and Recovery Directorate to manage unexpected crises, as well as known peaks in demands. It is made up of current and former civil servants who join on a voluntary basis and can be surged into particular roles in times of need.

How to join the Civil Service Reserve

If you are a current or former civil servant, you can register to join the Civil Service Reserve.

How to use the Civil Service Reserve

Currently, the Civil Service Reserve is only open to use for the Civil Contingencies Secretariat, National Cyber Force or Resilience and Recovery Directorate. If you are a member of one of those organisations, you can sign into the Civil Service Reserve platform.
